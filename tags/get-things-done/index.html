    Quora - What are effective ways to assess if someone is good at "getting things done"?

    November 15, 2014 get things done quora career

    Ian McAllister, GM at Amazon. I lead the AmazonSmile program.

    First, let’s break “getting things done” into two components: “excellent functional skills” and “drive”. Someone with great functional skills but poor drive will not get (enough) things done. Someone with great drive but poor functional skills will not get things done, or they’ll do the wrong things, or they’ll do the right things …
